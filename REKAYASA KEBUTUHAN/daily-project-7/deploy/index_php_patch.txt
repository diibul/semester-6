Open the file `public/index.php` (in your local project) and replace the autoload and bootstrap lines near the top with the following lines.

Replace these two lines (original):
    require __DIR__.'/../vendor/autoload.php';
    $app = require_once __DIR__.'/../bootstrap/app.php';

With these (for InfinityFree when `laravel_app` is sibling):
    require __DIR__.'/../laravel_app/vendor/autoload.php';
    $app = require_once __DIR__.'/../laravel_app/bootstrap/app.php';

If after uploading the app to `laravel_app` your public files are in `public_html`, this change makes `index.php` load the framework files from the sibling folder.
